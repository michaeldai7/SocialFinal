Chapter 3

Important Conferences on World Climate Change

World Climate Conference

The World Climate Conferences are a series of international meetings, organized by the World Meteorological Organization (WMO), about global climate issues principally global warming in addition to climate research and forecasting.

United Nations Climate Change conference

The United Nations Climate Change Conferences are yearly conferences held in the framework of the United Nations Framework Convention on Climate Change (UNFCCC).

Important Meetings

1997: COP 3, Kyoto, Japan

2000: COP 6, The Hague, Netherlands

2005: COP 11/CMP 1, Montreal, Canada
